Snake

A from-scratch browser recreation of original monochrome Nokia Snake: a 1-bit 84×48 display, tiny 2×2 pixel movement, score strip, bordered playfield, numbered-key controls, lethal walls, self-collision, and one-point food.

3210 note: The 3210-era game was the original Snake: number-pad steering inside a walled screen, collecting orbs while avoiding walls and your own body. This remake is clean-room browser code, not extracted Nokia firmware.

Controls: arrow keys, WASD, classic 2/4/6/8 number keys, Space to pause/start, or the phone buttons.
